### Claude Computer Use — Claude Now Controls Your Mac (Launched 3/23)

Anthropic launched **Claude Computer Use** on macOS. Claude can now directly operate your Mac to complete tasks.

**Key features:**
- **Direct control**: Claude opens apps, navigates browsers, fills spreadsheets, and edits files using mouse clicks and keyboard input
- **Connector-first**: For supported services (Google Workspace, Slack, etc.), connectors are prioritized over direct control
- **Permission requests**: Claude asks for user approval before sensitive actions (Anthropic recommends caution with sensitive information)
- **Dispatch integration**: Instruct Claude from your phone → tasks execute on your Mac for a complete remote workflow
- **Availability**: Claude Pro and Max subscribers (research preview)

[9to5Mac](https://9to5mac.com/2026/03/23/anthropic-is-giving-claude-the-ability-to-use-your-mac-for-you/) | [Engadget](https://www.engadget.com/ai/claude-code-and-cowork-can-now-use-your-computer-210000126.html) | [Blockchain News](https://blockchain.news/news/anthropic-claude-computer-control-mac-dispatch)
