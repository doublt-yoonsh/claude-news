## New Features & Practical Usage

### Anthropic Files Two Federal Lawsuits Against Trump Administration — Challenges "Supply Chain Risk" Designation (3/9)

Anthropic filed **two federal lawsuits** on Monday, March 9, to overturn the Pentagon's "supply chain risk" designation. One was filed in the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of California; the other in the U.S. Court of Appeals for the D.C. Circuit.

**Key issues:**
- The Pentagon designated Anthropic as a supply chain risk after the company refused to grant **unrestricted military use** of its technology
- Anthropic maintains two red lines: no **mass domestic surveillance** and no **fully autonomous lethal weapons**
- The Pentagon insists it cannot allow a private company to dictate usage in national security emergencies
- The designation — typically reserved for entities associated with **foreign adversaries** — affects Anthropic's ability to do business with defense contractors

**Anthropic's legal arguments:**
In a 48-page complaint, Anthropic called the actions "unprecedented and unlawful," arguing the government is **punishing the company for protected speech** in violation of the Constitution. The Treasury and State Departments are also named as defendants after ordering employees to stop using Anthropic services.

### Claude Marketplace Launches — A New Procurement Channel for Enterprise AI Tools (3/6)

Anthropic launched the **Claude Marketplace** in limited preview — a B2B marketplace where enterprise customers can use a portion of their existing API spending commitments to purchase Claude-powered third-party applications.

**Key features:**
- **Zero commission** — Anthropic takes no cut from third-party transactions
- Similar model to AWS and Azure marketplaces, but specialized for the AI-native ecosystem
- Consolidates vendor procurement into a **single channel** instead of dozens of contracts
- Launch partners: **GitLab, Harvey, Lovable, Replit, Rogo, Snowflake**

The goal is to simplify SaaS procurement, though concerns about **vendor lock-in** have been raised. Anthropic describes it as giving customers "more flexibility with spend they've already committed."

## Developer Workflow Tips

### CLAUDE.md Is Now as Essential as .gitignore

Here are the top best practices Claude Code experts consistently recommend in 2026.

**1. Plan first, code later:**
- Iterate on your plan in Plan mode, then switch to auto-accept only after approval
- "Never letting Claude write code until you've reviewed and approved a written plan is the **single most important thing**"

**2. Context hygiene:**
- Start fresh sessions per task, use `/clear` between unrelated tasks
- Compact proactively at 70% context usage
- Delegate research to subagents to protect your main context window

**3. Give verification criteria:**
- "Giving Claude verification criteria is the **single highest-leverage practice**" — performance improves dramatically when Claude can run tests, compare screenshots, and validate outputs itself

**4. Custom commands:**
- Custom commands in `.claude/commands/` are the **biggest productivity boost** — turn multi-step workflows like `/new-feature`, `/deploy`, `/test-all` into single shortcuts

A well-configured project (CLAUDE.md + custom commands + proper prompting) can ship features **5–10x faster** than a vanilla setup.

## Security & Limitations

### Three Chinese AI Labs Ran "Industrial-Scale" Distillation Attacks on Claude — 24K Fake Accounts, 16M Interactions (2/23)

Anthropic published evidence of large-scale model distillation campaigns by **DeepSeek, Moonshot AI, and MiniMax**. This incident provides important context for the Pentagon dispute.

**Scale of attack:**
- **24,000 fraudulent accounts** using commercial proxy services
- Over **16 million interactions** with Claude
- Each lab attributed via IP correlation, request metadata, and infrastructure indicators

**Per-lab targeting:**
- **DeepSeek**: Reasoning capabilities, rubric-based grading, censorship-safe alternatives for politically sensitive queries (150K+ interactions)
- **Moonshot AI**: Agentic reasoning, tool use, coding, computer vision (3.4M+ interactions)
- **MiniMax**: Agentic coding and tool use (13M+ interactions)

Anthropic warned that illicitly distilled models may lack safety guardrails and "could enable authoritarian governments to deploy frontier AI for offensive cyber operations, disinformation campaigns, and mass surveillance."
